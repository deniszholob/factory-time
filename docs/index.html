About

The universe has reached its terminal stage. Stars have died, black holes are evaporating, and thermodynamic equilibrium approaches. Your factory exists in a pocket of remaining usable energy, extracting resources from exotic sources: Hawking radiation, vacuum fluctuations, quantum foam, and the last traces of energy in spacetime itself.

Build production chains, automate everything, and unlock powerful technologies. Manage entropy accumulation—production increases universal entropy, making future operations harder. Choose when to reset based on diminishing returns versus prestige rewards.
